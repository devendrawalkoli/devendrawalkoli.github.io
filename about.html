Hi! My name is Devendra Walkoli, a graphic designer, illustrator, Web UI designer and Video/Photo Editor based in Pune. I specialise in digital art works, illustrations, posters, ads, logo, web design, Infographics, Video editing, Small Animations and what you ask for. Your imagination is my limit.

I completed my education in Computer Engineering. I got interested in graphics design, so I'm pursuing Graphic design with my full strength. As an engineer, it gives me an extra edge on completing all tasks more carefully and more organized.

I offer design services to business of all sizes and of all kinds. Most recently I've worked with startup in digital marketing. I've also worked with many small and big businesses like hotels, laundries, advertisements etc.

Goals

Main goals and objectives are to design a project based on the individual needs of a client. I must utilize my skills to determine physical, cultural and cognitive factors when designing a project to ensure a positive response for the target audience.

Goals

1 - Designs

I must provide designs for the client throughout the designing phase. This may be done through computer software programs or by hand. These tasks provide clients with detailed visual information regarding the development of the design. Throughout the design process, one must choose font styles, artwork, colour schemes and other elements for a project.

2 - Meeting Deadlines

The most important objective is to meet deadlines established by the client. As a designer, I must have a solid understanding of the amount of time it will take to successfully complete a project, and include enough extra time for any requested revisions from the client.

3 - Maintaining Budget

I must work within the budget established by the client. This ties into time-management skills, as a graphic designer, I must successfully complete a project within the time frame and budget set forth by the client. Missing a deadline may mean going over budget
